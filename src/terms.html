Description of Service

Innerval is a free, browser-based personal values discovery application. The Service includes:

  • Values Quiz — rate real-life scenarios across five life areas (Work, Relationships, Personal Growth, Social Impact, and Leisure) at three depth levels (Essentials, Deep Dive, or Full Spectrum) to generate a ranked values profile
  • Results & Export — view your values ranked in five tiers, download a PDF report, or export your data as a JSON file
  • Explore Values — browse all values with descriptions, examples, and categories, and build a personal aspirations list
  • Compare Profiles — upload two exported JSON files to see an alignment score, radar chart, side-by-side rankings, and detailed breakdown
  • Growth Plan — generate a structured prompt based on your results and aspirations that you can paste into any AI assistant for personalised guidance

All processing occurs entirely within your browser — no personal data is sent to or stored on any server we operate.
