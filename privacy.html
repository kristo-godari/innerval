Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Overview

Innerval ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our personal values discovery quiz at innerval.app.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays in your browser.

Information We Do Not Collect

Innerval is a fully client-side application. We do not:

  • Require you to create an account or sign in
  • Collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Send your quiz answers or results to any server
  • Use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes
  • Integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services

Local Storage

Innerval uses your browser's localStorage feature to save your quiz progress so you can resume where you left off. This data:

  • Is stored entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server
  • Can be cleared at any time by using the "Restart Test" button or clearing your browser data
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party

No Server-Side Processing

All quiz logic, scoring, and result calculations happen entirely within your browser. No data leaves your device at any point during or after taking the quiz.

Third-Party Services

Innerval does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, social media tracking, or data collection. The application consists solely of static files served to your browser.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The quiz is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we do not collect contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
