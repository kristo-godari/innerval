About This Quiz

Understanding your personal values is one of the most powerful tools for self-awareness. Values are the fundamental beliefs that guide your behaviour, decisions, and sense of fulfilment.

This quiz presents you with 310 real-life scenarios spanning 62 universally recognised values — from Acceptance and Courage to Wisdom and Adventure. By rating how each scenario resonates with you, you build an authentic map of what matters most in your life.

Whether you're navigating a career change, improving relationships, or simply seeking clarity, knowing your values gives you a compass for every decision.

Why Innerval Exists

If you've ever searched for a personal values test online, you know the frustration. Most websites only cover a handful of values, ask you a dozen vague questions, and then claim to know what drives you. We tried them — many of them — and walked away feeling like the results barely scratched the surface.

The problem? Shallow questions produce shallow answers. A quick quiz with generic prompts like "How important is honesty to you?" doesn't push you to really think. You end up rating everything as important and learning nothing new about yourself.

That's exactly why we built Innerval. Yes, 300+ situational questions is a lot — and that's by design. Each scenario places you in a concrete, real-life moment so your answers come naturally, from instinct rather than overthinking. It's the difference between being asked "Do you value courage?" and being shown a situation where courage actually matters — and seeing how you honestly respond.

Take your time with it. There's no rush, and your progress saves automatically. The depth is what makes the results meaningful — and what finally gives you a values profile you can actually trust.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the quiz take?

If you complete all 62 values it takes roughly 30–45 minutes, but you can skip values and return to them later. Your progress is saved automatically.

Is my data stored anywhere?

No. All answers are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server — your data never leaves your device.

Can I retake the quiz?

Absolutely. Use the Restart button at any time to clear your progress and start fresh.

Do I have to complete all 62 values?

No — you can end the test early and see partial results for the values you've completed. However, a full profile gives you the most accurate ranking.

What do the tiers in the results mean?

Values are grouped into five tiers based on your scores: Core Values (top ~16%), Very Important, Important, Moderate, and Less Important. The tiers help you see which values are truly central to who you are.

Can I download my results?

Yes! Once you finish the quiz, you can download your complete values profile as a PDF. It's a great way to save your results, revisit them later, or share them with a coach or therapist.

How does the comparison feature work?

Each person takes the quiz and exports their results as a JSON file using the "Export Data" button. Then either of you can upload both files on the Compare page to see an alignment score, a radar chart of top values, side-by-side rankings, strongest similarities, biggest differences, a tier comparison table, and a category breakdown — all downloadable as a PDF.
